Audit

In an increasingly restrictive environment, the quality of financial information is a guarantee of security as well as a powerful communication tool, whether the certification of your accounts or the attestation of the performance of your internal control. Based on a recognized audit approach adapted to the size of your company, Asma Audit & Conseil assists SMEs and large companies in the certification of accounts or in other more specific missions. For this purpose, you wish:

obtain legal certification of your accounts

attest to the reliability of your financial statements

carry out interventions governed by the law - certificates, capital transactions, issue of instruments

Our solutions

The legal audit can attest to the quality of accounting and financial information but also to prevent and reduce risks through the assessment of procedures. Beyond the audit, it's about evaluating the effectiveness of an organization. The auditing of the firm and the experience of our employees ensure that our clients benefit for statutory audit assignments under optimum conditions and with due diligence.
